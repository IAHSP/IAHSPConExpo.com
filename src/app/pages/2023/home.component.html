IAHSP Professional Stager Continuing Education
IAHSP CONEXPO is your GETAWAY with a TAKEAWAY!

As our industry has grown, so has the need to establish professional standards for education and business practices. Since the pathway into staging has not mandated education or adherence to higher standards, we decided to develop an industry-wide Continuing Education program for Pro-Stagers that will accomplish two KEY goals:

  1. It will allow stagers to be up to date on the important aspects of our industry
  2. Let the public know what to look for in hiring a Pro-Stager

Pro-Stager Continuing Education provides:

  • Up to date info on the real estate market trends, where we are headed for the next 12 months, and how that impacts our clients and our business
  • Up to date education on the latest design and staging trends for color, furniture styles, materials, artwork, decor, rugs, etc.
  • Up to date education on important issues concerning our industry including but not limited to: Pricing Strategies, Costs, Hiring Strategies, Excellence Standards, and Code of Ethics, Regulatory issues

Upon completion, you will receive: Your IAHSP® Pro Stager CE Certificate and Logo.

To receive these new credentials, attendees must attend all sessions assigned to this designation.

This IAHSP-PSCE requires annual education and passing a test to retain status as a Pro-Stager.

This is JENNIE NORRIS SHARING:

The Home Staging industry's NEWEST Credential is the PSCE - Professional Stager Continuing Education recipient. This logo will be earned by those in the home staging industry who want to share with their potential clients that they are up to date on real estate market trends, design and style trends, and code of ethics, pricing and business practices to serve and protect their clients.

It will be offered for the FIRST TIME at the 2023 IAHSP ConEXPO in New Orleans, and all attendees will receive this new credential.

WHY?

Just about every professional has CE requirements to keep a license and continue practice. Real estate agents need to attend CE courses every year. Home Staging should be no different. The work we do to impact the industry is critical, and we want to be seen as a mandatory part of the process, not an option, and not a service that people think is just about putting anything in a house and calling it "staged."

Truth is, the Home Staging does not have a MANDATORY path for having a business and serving the public. There is no class required, no test to take and no mandatory continuing education - and this was a MISTAKE that needs to be corrected. People who want to be part of this industry need to understand expectations, be held to a performance standard and understand pricing and value for the services we offer.

Professionals serving this industry SHOULD have to be up to date on the industry they serve - real estate - and understand how the industry has EVOLVED so they are not doing things that went away 15-20 years ago, and there are stagers who continue to diminish the professionalism of our industry by thinking they are staging, when in reality they are putting some small decor and accent pieces in a vacant house, and THAT is NOT staging. They were not taught standards, and do not know what they do not know. But we do.

We need to match the expectations of BUYERS - where over 80% of them EXPECT houses for sale to LOOK LIKE THEY DO ON TV - on HGTV - where they are stylized and professionally Staged. We need to increase the standards for our services, and the ONLY way to accomplish that is to require continuing education for industry members.

Those who choose not to obtain this credential are therefore choosing not to be the most up-to-date, accountable, and adhere to standards for a professional stager in the industry, and the public needs to know who to avoid and who to hire. Rather than go find the info on your own, attend a course that is inexpensive, and ensures you have the most accurate and timely information.

The course will be offered virtually online and annual continued education requirements will be in place from IAHSP, the ONLY professional industry association that requires a baseline of education to join. #StandardsMatter.

Annual CE will be $99 and will ensure all recipients and Pro-Stagers are up to date on important issues impacting our industry. For 2023 attendees, the PSCE is INCLUDED in your conference registration payment.
